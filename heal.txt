The waters of the eternal spring will rise and wash over my wounds. I feel their healing power, rich in mana.
My mother taught me how to heal the wounds of those who are suffering. Every day I apply her wisdom to help myself and others.
Alone, and weary, I hear nothing and feel nothing. Suddenly a spark from above fills the air with life. I feel my senses returning.
A simple herb from the Shepherd Bush, crushed into powder and mixed with the root of the Fig Tree. This potion heals swiftly.
A shot to my constitution, a boon from the gods. A flash of light and my health increases. I will give offerings in thanks for this gift.
In my darkest hour I find the power to survive. A small pin point of light grows larger. I am healed.
